Exploring the La Digue

The La Digue is one of Africa’s greatest Islands in Seychelles. I usually visit during the summer seasons...

The experience of seeing thousands of wildebeest cross the Mara River is breathtaking. We also spotted lions, elephants, and cheetahs on our game drives. The local Maasai people were incredibly welcoming, and their traditions made the trip even more enriching.

Backpacking Through Morocco

Morocco is a land of diverse culture, spices, and endless adventure. Here’s my experience through the souks and dunes...

Starting from Marrakech, I wandered through narrow alleys filled with spices, carpets, and leather goods. The Sahara desert was the highlight — riding camels at sunset and camping under the stars is something I’ll never forget.
